# The Death of Ivan Ilych

**The Death of Ivan Ilych** is a novel by [Leo Tolstoy](../notes/leo-tolstoy).

This book likely influenced the making of [Stalker (1979)](stalker.md) ([20240529050749](../entries/20240529050749.md)).
