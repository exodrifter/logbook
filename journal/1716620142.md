Doing a retrospective on what it was like to use [Logseq](logseq) today.

It feels like I will have a much easier time writing devlogs in the future, especially as the task of making images is already done, a task that often feels really tedious when I try to find a good picture of something I've done later when writing a devlog.

I also like the task system in Logseq, and that seems to be working well for me. It's nice to jot down something I want to do and get a collated list of it at the bottom of the note.

I might need to migrate some of my old log entries to this system.
