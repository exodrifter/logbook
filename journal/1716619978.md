Discussing two different ways to end [no signal](no_signal) with Tanuki:

- Original ending: When you fix the station, you find yourself on the Dutchman in first person, at which point the game tries to get you to actually fix the station for real.
- New ending: When you fix the station, you have the choice of either stabilizing the orbit (which loops the game back to the beginning) or destabilizing it into the black hole (which gives you the true ending, where you receive the FTL plans to leave). This risks players missing the narrative payoff, but on the other hand it rewards player agency more.
- The two endings or not that different from each other, so we're going to try for the new ending and see how players react in playtesting. If it doesn't work, then we can retool the game into the original ending without too much effort.
